Trees

When picking a tree what i feel is the most important question is, do i buy a fake tree or get a real one. Lets figure out some advantages too both.

Artificial Trees vs Real Trees

For me personally the artificial tree is the way to go. It has many advantages over its real deal like ; not needed to be watered, no loose needles needed to be swept up , and also some come with lights already installed. While the artificial tree seems like the better choice nothing can beat the joy of having a real tree. From going with the family to get the tree, the decorating with family and putting the star at the top.

Gifts

When making gift purchases it can be nerve raking if you do not get it right. There is a better way to handle it

Surprise gifts or make a list and share with family

When buying gifts for family members it can be hard. You may feel that you may disappoint them if you do not get it right. On the other hand when you do get it right it feels amazing. Now a days though i see a lot more family or extended family like friends using apps that just shares what you want to take the guess work out of it. If your family prefers just buy what you feel the other person may like. I like to throw in the receipt just in case and not feel bad if i got it wrong.
