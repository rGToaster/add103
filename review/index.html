Diving in deeper

From what has been stated before this game is more that just survival it is a open world sandbox game. What you in a sandbox you create, destroy, have adventures, and most important of all have fun! When it comes to creating you have a wide variety of blocks that let you create large structures from recreating the statue of liberty too a simple shack that you decide to live in made out of dirt blocks. If that not your style, you have the option of redstone. Redstone is minecraft version of electricity allowing you too power large machines that you build in minecraft too either make your life easier or harder for your enemy to attack you by making auto tnt cannons. I have also scene people use the redstone to make be able to play minecraft in minecraft. It is truly a fascinating game that only your imagination is the limit.
