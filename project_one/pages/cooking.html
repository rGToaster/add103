Pozole

Pozole, the national stew of mexico. I say national but in reality every region in Mexico has its own twist on the stew. To keep it brief we can categorize the pozole into three categories: pozole rojo (red), pozole verde (green) and pozole blanco (plain). Typically each one has its die hard fans that believe the other two are inferior to their favorites. The color comes from what chilies and tomatoes you used for the base of the sauce. For green you use serranos or jalapenos and green tomatillos(green tomatoes). While Red pozole gets its color from tomatoes and using guajillo, piquin or hancho. Last but not the least pozole blanco which forgoes the tomatoes and chillies typically the choice for children who can be picky as it is not spicy and simple. Also, just like tacos the toppings are how you make it unique to you like:

  • chicharon
  • cabbage
  • habanero chillie slices
  • cilantro
  • oregano
  • lime
  • onions
