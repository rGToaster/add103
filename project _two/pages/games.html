Top 5 games at the moment that I actually play

Everyone has their go-to games, and these five are the ones I keep coming back to. Anytime I have time to kill is typically spent playing one of these 5 games. I like to play sand box or casual games, so at the moment it has made this list a bit biased.

Star sector the game that simulates a galaxy that has been caught in a struggle between many factions to consolidate power to conquer the whole galaxy. Your mission is too either help on e of the 8 main factions to control the galaxy or start your own colony and grow to rival that of the other faction. With the ned goal to conquer the galaxy yourself.

​Webfishing is a cozy indie fishing game developed by lamedeveloper, released on Steam in October 2024. Players control customizable animal avatars in a 3D world, engaging in activities like fishing, metal detecting, and playing a virtual guitar. The game emphasizes social interaction, allowing 1–12 players to chat and relax together. Its simple mechanics and charming aesthetics have drawn comparisons to Animal Crossing and Club Penguin. Webfishing has received overwhelmingly positive reviews for its relaxing gameplay and community-focused design.

Stardew Valley is a charming farming simulation game where players inherit a rundown farm and work to restore it. You can grow crops, raise animals, fish, mine, and build relationships with townsfolk. With its relaxing gameplay, pixel art style, and rich customization options, it offers a peaceful escape and a sense of community. The game also includes seasonal events, crafting, and the option to marry and start a family. Whether you're exploring caves or planting strawberries, Stardew Valley provides a satisfying blend of strategy, creativity, and heartwarming moments.

League of Legends is a fast-paced, competitive online multiplayer game developed by Riot Games. Players form teams of five and compete to destroy the opposing team’s Nexus, the core building in their base. Each player selects a unique champion with distinct abilities, leading to deep strategic gameplay and constant action. With over 160 champions, frequent updates, and a massive global esports scene, League of Legends remains one of the most popular and influential games in the world. Its blend of teamwork, mechanics, and strategy challenges players to improve and adapt every match.

Minecraft is a sandbox adventure game that lets players explore, build, and survive in a blocky, procedurally generated world. With modes like Creative and Survival, it offers endless possibilities—from constructing massive structures to fighting off monsters and gathering resources. Its simple graphics hide deep mechanics, including redstone circuitry, enchantments, and custom mods. Whether played solo or with friends, Minecraft fosters creativity, problem-solving, and exploration. Its global popularity and active community have made it a cultural icon and a staple in both gaming and education.
